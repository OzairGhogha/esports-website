Sunday 30th August 2020

Dallas Empire win Call of Duty Championship 2020

Today Dallas Empire showed a commanding performance against Atlanta FaZe to win the 2020 Championship. Despite being held online this year due to COVID-19 the 2020 Championship was still watched by thousands worldwide. Dallas Empire beat Atlanta FaZe 5-1 to win an impressive $1,500,000

Thursday 7th January 2021

Raid added to CoD Map Pool

The new Call of Duty competitive settings update has brought an iconic map into the new rotation. Raid was first seen in Treyarchs' 2012 Black Ops 2 and was a instant success. The map was extremely popular due it being both fun to watch and play on compared to others. The 2021 Call of Duty League will now use this map for its teams to play the Hardpoint, Search and Destoy and Control modes.

Wednesday 11th November 2020

Optic Chigago Roster Confirmed

OpTic Gaming Chigago have revealed their 2021 roster to compete in the latest Call of Duty title: Cold War. Superstar Seth 'Scump' Abner joins Matthew 'Formal' Piper, Dylan "Envoy" Hannon and Brandon 'Dashy' Otell to complete the roster. This team is clearly stacked with talent, but will they be able to perform as expected?
