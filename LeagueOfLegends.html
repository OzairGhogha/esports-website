The lastest news from the world of eSports!

Friday 20th November 2020

Rekkles Joins G2 eSports

G2 Esports have signed iconic bot laner Martin 'Rekkles' Larsson after an impressive 2020 Worlds performance with Fnatic. This now finalises G2's LEC roster after the departure of Luka 'Perkz' Perkovic to North American LCS Spring Split champions Cloud 9 Esports. This move reunites Caps and Rekkles after their successful year together on Fnatic in 2018. This move marks the end of Rekkles' long 8 year tenure at Fnatic. “My goal has always been to prove myself as one of the most successful players in the game, and despite having 4 EU titles and a Worlds silver medal, I am not satisfied, It is with this in mind that I have joined G2. They are a great organisation and a great brand. Winning Worlds is as much of an ultimate goal for them as it is for me. So, when they asked me to join, it was a no-brainer.” Rekkles said. “Rekkles will create his own path here and I know the G2ARMY will support him along the way. He promised he would win Worlds. And he will. With G2,” said G2 owner Carlos ‘ocelote’ Rodríguez Santiago.

Friday 20th November 2020

Perkz Joins Cloud 9

Cloud 9 has confirmed the signing of Luka 'Perkz' Perkovic from G2 Esports. In one of the biggest western League of Legends transfers of all time Perkz will shift his talents overseas to North America to replace former C9 midlaner Nisqy.

Tuesday 24th November 2020

Nisqy joins Fnatic

Belgian midlaner Yasin 'Nisqy' Dinçer has returned to Europe to play for Fnatic in 2021. After the departure of former mid laner Tim "Nemesis" Lipovšek Fnatic were desperate for a suitable replacement. Nisqy is now returning to the LEC after spending 2 years in the LCS at Cloud 9 where he managed to win the 2020 Spring Split.

Thursday 26th November 2020

Upset Joins Fnatic

Today Elias 'Upset' Lipp signed a 3 year deal with Fnatic. Upset has now completed a move from Origen where he played AD-Carry for a year, and showcased his talent. After the departure of star Martin 'Rekkles' Larsson Upset has big shoes to fill. Fnatic support 'Hylissang' said: “I'm really excited to play with Upset. He’s one of the few AD carries that can match my playstyle, and I can't wait to see how we’re going to work together on the Rift.”

Friday 20th November 2020

Alphari Joins Team Liquid

Origens star top laner Barney 'Alphari' Morris has completed a move to the NA LCS. He will join Team Liquid as the starting top laner for the 2021 season. Alphari tweeted "Does mean that I won't competing in the LEC next year. Despite the last few year's results I've loved my time in Europe, and deciding to change to NA was definitely not an easy decision. Also can understand how some fans will be disappointed in seeing me leave EU, - I just hope you'll be able to support me regardless as I aim to prove myself internationally again next year. This will be my time to achieve results."
